Simply Ileta

I was born in Portland, Oregon in a home with my 5 brothers and sisters. My first language I learned was Russian, so entering school was a bit challenging fo me. Fast forward ahead and now I can barely speak any Russian! I guess it's true what they say, "If you don't use it, you lose it".

I moved to the Big Island of Hawaii a few years after graduating from high school and that was probably the highlight of my life. I met some amazing locals and works at some fun jobs! Although I am not sure I would ever move back, but I definitely would love to visit and vacation there.

What am I doing these days?

I work at a beautiful third-wave coffe house in Vancouver, Washington. It is called Gold Cup Coffee House, and we take our espresso seriously! We want to make sure that the gold is in everyones cup. This job is fun and lighthearted, but I definitely am ready to find my career!

I am currently attending a rad boot camp called Epicodus! It is a fully immersive coding school where you work closely with your peers, even though youre learning remotely! It is definitely tough, but extremely rewarding.

I have been a vegan for about 5 and a half years! It started out as an economic thing, but it stuck around because I realized a lot of my health issues were resolved. But now? I do it because I cannot imagine eating another animal product ever again! No thank you!
